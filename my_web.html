🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle your data with care and transparency.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you register, place an order, or contact us. We also automatically collect usage data via cookies to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us process orders, personalize recommendations, send updates, and enhance our services. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. All transactions are processed through secure gateways.

4. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Opt out of marketing communications with one click.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out to us at hanan@hananandco.com.
